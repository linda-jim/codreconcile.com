Our Mission

To empower D2C brands by transforming Cash on Delivery (COD) into a reliable, efficient, and scalable payment option.
We aim to bridge the operational and technological gaps in COD with smart, data-driven solutions that reduce RTOs, improve order verification, and boost customer trust. This will help merchants thrive in emerging markets where COD still drives growth.

Our Story

We are redefining the COD experience for Shopify merchants. Our innovative application bridges the gap in Shopify’s native COD functionality, offering seamless solutions that improve efficiency, reduce revenue leakages, and boost conversions. We empower e-commerce businesses with smart COD tools like advanced order verification, automated follow-ups, and real-time analytics so you can focus on scaling while we handle the complexity of COD.

We are a team of builders, problem-solvers, and e-commerce enthusiasts on a mission to simplify COD for D2C brands. With backgrounds in product development, payment systems, and logistics automation, we’ve come together to solve one of the biggest pain points in high-growth markets — making COD work better for merchants and their customers.

Our founders have firsthand experience scaling D2C brands and understand the operational friction COD can cause. Our developers and product teams build clean, scalable solutions that work. From engineers to customer success, every member of our team shares one goal; and that's helping you convert more orders, reduce returns, and grow with confidence.

Trusted by growing brands, we're committed to helping merchants succeed in markets where COD remains essential. Transparency, speed, and delivering real value are at the core of our approach. Join us on this journey to transform the COD landscape. Whether you're a small business or a large enterprise, our solutions are designed to scale with you.
